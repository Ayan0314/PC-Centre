Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you provide warranty on products?

Yes, all products come with manufacturer warranty. Additionally, we offer extended warranty options at the time of purchase for extra protection.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within the city. Express delivery is available for next-day delivery on selected items.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for desktops, networking equipment, and enterprise solutions. Home installation is available for consumers, and on-site installation for businesses.

What payment method do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and cash on delivery for orders under $500.

Can I upgrade components in my existing computer?

Absolutely! Our technical team can help you choose compatible upgrades and perform the installation. Bring your PC to our service center or schedule a home visit.

Do you provide technical support after purchase?

Yes, we have a dedicated team of Technical Support Advisors available via phone, email, and in-person at our service centers. Support is included for all products purchased from PC-Centre.

Are refurbished products available?

Yes, we offer certified refurbished products at discounted prices. All refurbished items are thoroughly tested and come with a 90-day warranty.

Still Need Help?

Our dedicated team of Technical Support and Customer Service Advisors is ready to assist you with any product inquiries, technical issues, or service requests. We aim to exceed your expectations with superior after-sales support.
